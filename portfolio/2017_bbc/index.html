BBC Digital Cities

Working with an ex-BBC colleague, I helped to organise a one day B2B conference for the BBC Digital Cities programme.

Using my network within the games community, I arranged a programme that is unique in the Scottish gaming sector in being aimed at professional peers rather than being student facing.

We brought together publishers, creative directors, founders, funders and other professionals to discuss practical challenges facing the Scottish videogames community.
