#GamesInsight

For the last several years, I’ve organised a series of high profile invited talks in Glasgow. The #GamesInsight series has managed to attract steady interest from game developers and students across Scotland. By hand-selcting individual speakers, and only organising a talk when an appropriate speaker was available, I’ve managed to create an association of quality and an element of prestige to these talks.

Previous speakers have included the founder of Xbox Live and XNA, Boyd Multerer (pictured), Jon McKellan (BAFTA Breakthrough Brit and founder of NoCode), Fraser MacLean (author and former Disney animator), and Michael Brough (innovative game designer).
