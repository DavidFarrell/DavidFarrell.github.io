Brand Bang!

I designed and developed a Faceboook and iPad game that would perform market research whilst people played. Brand Bang is a game where players remove tiles to reveal a logo underneath. Players attempt to guess the logo in as few moves as possible. The data collected this way allowed Bunnyfood to determine the brand strength and characteristics of log identity. It was used to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns by comparing how many moves players required before and after the campaign.
