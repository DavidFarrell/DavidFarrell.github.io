Game Design Consultancy

I like to keep my hand in with professional game design and I offer private game consultancy to development studios. In recent years, several of the games I have credits on have achieved acclaim. The form and style of contribution depends on the needs and expertise of the project team and my other commitments.

To illustrate, on the two BAFTA winning NoCode titles I helped with (Observation and Stories Untold) I mainly contributed general design consultancy throughout the duration of developmnt. On the Channel 4 published title, Party Pugs, I contributed to writing and level design.

Games I have helped with have been published on almost all platforms from mobile and Facebook games to Playstation, Xbox and Switch.
