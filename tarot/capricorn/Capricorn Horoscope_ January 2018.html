Capricorn Horoscope: January 2018

Rock solid
last month this month next month

2018 will be a pivotal year for you, Capricorn, and it'll become clear why as soon as February opens up. On Febraury 1 a sensitive Full Moon will fall in your partnership sector. This lunation might bring the most remarkable fulfillment your way in a relationship. But be wary, as the lunation may lead you astray. This February watch for moments of disagreement between you and your romantic partner. Taking a minor disagreement towards a fight is ill advised as only a very patient friend will not respond in kind. Don't be an ass Capricorn. Your beauty, charm, sense of humour, stunning intelligence, outrageous wit, impeccable taste, sharp mind, and unparallelled empathy does not give you permission to be a tool.
